GUIDELINE TO HALT MORATORIUM PERIOD

Dear kreateng, If you are currently employed but your loan is still within it's moratorium period we advise that you halt your moratorium and begin repayment, this is due to the fact that as you already know your loan accrues interest during the moratorium period. Doing this would help you pay off your loan faster thereby reducing the total amount paid on interest. If you would like to halt your moratorium kindly follow the below steps:

  • kindly refer HERE for the document title Request to halt Moratorium Sterling Bank, download it and adapt it, to suit your request ensure you look out for the dates and update appropriately also make sure that you sign. Send an email to decagonsupport@sterling.ng cc Chiamaka.ibekwe@sterling.ng and fredricae@decagonhq.com requesting to halt your moratorium.
  • Kindly include a copy of your employment offer letter to assist the bank ascertain that you are currently in employment.
  • Your request will be reviewed and feedback provided to you. If you don't receive fee any communication within 48 working hours of sending the email please feel free to send a reminder.
